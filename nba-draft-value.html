How to read this chart

Each dot is a draft pick. Dots above the curve are steals — players who outperformed their slot. Dots below the curve are busts. The orange curve shows the expected value at each pick based on historical averages. Picks in the top-10 have the highest expected value, but also the most variance — the difference between Giannis at #15 and Anthony Bennett at #1 is the whole story of draft evaluation.
